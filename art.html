Apple Casting Experiment

“He threw a golden apple... and she, marveling, slowed to pick it up.”

Ovid, Metamorphoses, Book X

In Greek mythology, the huntress Atalanta promised to marry any suitor who out-raced her. Using three golden apples to distract her, Hippomenes won her hand. His genius and the beauty of the apples proved victor over brute strength. This casting experiment was inspired by that story. My way of capturing allure and consequence in a single object.

Original 3D printed apple model

Polished bronze apple after casting

From Apple to Alloy
Attaching my wax apples to the tree
Encased in investment mold
Burning out the wax
Melting the Bronze
