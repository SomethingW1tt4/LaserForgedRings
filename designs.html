Interlocking Band Types – Unique Engineering

Thanks to advanced manufacturing, our rings can be made with two distinct interlocking mechanisms:

Traditional Spinner Style: The inner band features two visible lips on the outside, holding the outer band in place—just like a classic spinner ring.

Hidden Channel Innovation: Unique to our patented process, the interlocking channel is concealed inside the ring, between the bands. This allows for a seamless exterior and a smoother, more elegant feel.
