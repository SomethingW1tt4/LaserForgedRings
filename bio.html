From Philosophy to Fabrication

Outlining my education and achievements from liberal arts to laser-forged hardware.

Personal Statement

I’ve crossed disciplines and rewired my path — from a degree in Philosophy to precision engineering and digital fabrication. I believe that abstract reasoning and hands-on creation are two sides of the same coin. Whether designing tower facades in Rhino or debugging a C++ project at midnight, I’m always looking for elegant solutions to messy problems.

Education

A.S. Computer Science – OCC
with a Specialization in C++

Certifications

Leadership Certificate – OCC

Utility Patents

Previous Employment

I served as General Manager for California Imports, a specialty auto business, where I led operations, inventory management, and customer relations. I gained deep experience in small-business problem solving and client-facing communication.
